LA VIDA TECH SOLUTIONS

COMPANY PROFILE

History

La Vida Tech Solutions was established primarily to service a niche industry in software development. It seeks to establish itself as the premiere security-first and security conscious software consulting and development firm in Visayas, if not the Philippines.
As Present time, it is developing its own product and training key people to develop and drive the business.

Nature of Business

La Vida Tech Solutions is a software consulting and development company. It provides consulting work for software development projects and also produces software specified by clients from various industries
It also provides application security testing and network security testing in the form of penetrating testing.

Location

We are on the cusp of greatness or doom. We are nowhere and everywhere. We are where you are.
Of the 7,641 islands in the Philippines, we are situated in the technology friendly Cebu City. Central Visayas' business district and melting pot of excellent minds.

Process

At La Vida Tech Solutions, we strive to understand what the client's perspective is on the consulting and development of projects they seek our expertise with. As such, the process necessary to achieve such custom and personalized service is the adoption of agile practices. No specific agile practice is currently preferred but to name a few, we practice; Scrum, Extreme Programming, Lean Development, and Feature Driven Development.
