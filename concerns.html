OJT-TRAINEE CONCERNS

Things Learned During OJT

La Vida Tech Solutions gave me more room to improve and explore myself. In terms of work ethics, I have learned the advantages of punctuality in attendance and timeliness in projects. I have also learned how to maximize my skills and enhance my knowledge without being dependent with academic learnings or with other people. I learned how to work things on my own with patience that I can't learn everything in one blow. Yes, I do still ask from my mentors in the company but they encouraged me to try my hardest to come up solutions in my own way rather than be dependent on what they say. Mentors exist to guide, but it doesn't necessarily mean that we should do it like the way they did because there's always a room for us to learn things on our own.
On the other hand, I've been able to try and practice skills in different areas. I've been able to design web pages, study about security issues and learn working in different operating systems, such as Windows and Linux. I learned how to work with command lines and become less dependent to GUIs. I have also learned the usefulness of version control systems. And lastly, I learned the importance of securing my personal data from attacks.
