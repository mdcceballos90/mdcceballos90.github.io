DEPARTMENTS

MANAGEMENT & CONSULTING DIVISION

The Management & Consulting has more than 18 years of combined experience at the heart of it. Its duty involves attracting clients and solving problems clients have, with respect to software or software consulting needs, consequently La Vida Tech Solutions can develop the software necessary available/recommended existing software is not the clients specifications.

SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT DIVISION

The Software Development Division is our product development arm. It seeks agile individuals who are Operating Systems and/or Platform Agnostics who can adapt to the ever changing landscape of a Technology Driven society. The division works with technology stack apt for client's requirements. It could be a desktop solution for Windows or Linux in which C# .Net or QT/Java might be used respectively. It could be a web-based application with which a variety of development stacks can be used, like LAMPP, MEAN, etc.

OJT DUTY

The OJT's were made to be acquainted with general purpose tools used for web-based application development. On an almost daily basis, new tools and development roadblocks are added to their stacks for them to use and work-on. Their duty is to think like and become software developers & solutions provider.
Part of their duty is to learn and understand why certain security-conscious solution is favored over a simpler implementation. The desireed effect is that they learn how to think like a programmer and solve problems that concerns project stakeholders' needs without sacrificing application security and data integrity
